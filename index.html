About Me

Arizona isn't just heat

It is the state with the very best sunsets and road tripping views. You will never be disappointed with the earth around you. There might not be lots of color, but when you look for the details you'll see how beautiful the earth's marks are.

Great Hikes

Trails, full of animals and plants. A learning experience for all who are looking for a challenge. You'll work up a sweat but your hard work will pay off to see the gorgeous sun at the end.

Underated Experiences

The state plant, the Saguaro Cactus grows prickly pears so be careful when picking. But, they make incredible smoothies! In no other state are you told not to put sunscreen on your forehead beacuse you will sweat the sunscreen into your eyes. Always carry around a bottle and one for water too!
